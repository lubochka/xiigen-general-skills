# Checkpoint Model Lifecycle Template

## Intended Architecture
Use for projects with versioned checkpoints, models, generated artifacts, or rollback requirements.

## Project Inputs
Artifact types, storage, promotion flow, validation commands, rollback rules, retention policy.

## Adaptation Questions
- What counts as a checkpoint?
- What makes a version ready?
- Who can promote or rollback?
- What evidence is retained or redacted?

## Template Rules
Define lifecycle states, validation gates, promotion rules, rollback process, and artifact policy.

## Outputs
Checkpoint lifecycle guide, promotion checklist, rollback runbook, retention policy.

## Review Gates
Raw checkpoints, models, training data, and generated bundles remain out of reusable guidance.

## Non-Goals
Does not publish model artifacts or prescribe storage technology.
