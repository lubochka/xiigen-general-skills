# Trainable Architecture Template

## Intended Architecture
Use for projects that adapt behavior through feedback, versioned learning units, model artifacts, prompts, or training loops.

## Project Inputs
Domain, trainable unit type, feedback source, versioned artifacts, promotion gates, rollback policy.

## Adaptation Questions
- What is being trained or adapted?
- Who approves promotion?
- What evidence proves improvement?
- What artifacts must never be published?

## Template Rules
Define unit manifests, feedback intake, lifecycle states, promotion criteria, and evidence boundaries.

## Outputs
Project-specific trainable manifest, lifecycle guide, promotion checklist, and evidence contract.

## Review Gates
No mandatory source-specific product names, learning interface names, training acronyms, private paths, raw models, or training data.

## Non-Goals
Does not prescribe ML stack, model format, training framework, or runtime implementation.
