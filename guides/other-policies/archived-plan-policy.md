# Archived Plan Policy

Archived plans, old sessions, zips, and large bundles are historical source material.

## Rules
1. Mark archived content as historical.
2. Do not let archived plans override active docs.
3. Exclude raw archives and copied worktrees from public artifacts.
4. Extract reusable lessons only through source classification.

## Gates
Every archive reference says whether it is active, historical, excluded, or sanitized.
