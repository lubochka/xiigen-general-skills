# Evidence Artifact Policy

Evidence supports claims; evidence does not approve completion.

## Accepted Evidence
Target-project command output, smoke-test records, review records, sanitized screenshots, and file references.

## Unsafe Evidence
Secrets, credentials, local absolute paths, raw logs, copied worktrees, raw screenshots with sensitive data, and generated bundles.

## Gates
Evidence is redacted, scoped, and linked to the claim it supports.
